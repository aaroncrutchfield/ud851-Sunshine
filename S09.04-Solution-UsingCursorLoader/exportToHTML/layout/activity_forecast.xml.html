23         - This RecyclerView will be responsible for displaying our list of weather data. All of  - 
24         - its layout attributes should make sense to you, perhaps except for                     - 
25         - android:clipToPadding="false". When we set clipToPadding to false, we are telling      - 
26         - RecyclerView to not resize the over-scrolling effect that happens when you attempt to  - 
27         - scroll past the end of the content.                                                    - 
28         -                                                                                        - 
29         - Go ahead and test it out yourself. Set clipToPadding to "true" and scroll to the very  - 
30         - bottom of the list. Then, keep scrolling. You'll see the "glow" that happens. That is  - 
31         - called an EdgeEffect in Android. The problem when clipToPadding is set to "true" is    - 
32         - that there is a gap between the bottom of the screen and the EdgeEffect. Set           - 
33         - clipToPadding to "false" again and observe how the gap is gone.                        - 
34         - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -->
